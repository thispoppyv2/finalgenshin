Sumeru

Sumeru is a nation in Teyvat that's known as the center of learning. It's home to the Sumeru Akademiya, which is a fantastical nation with forests and deserts. Sumeru worships Lesser Lord Kusanali, the Dendro Archon and God of Wisdom. Sumeru, in Genshin Impact, is a land shrouded in mystery but pulsating with the promise of discovery. This nation, ruled by the Dendro Archon (god of life), prioritizes knowledge above all else, making it a haven for scholars and academics. Lush rainforests teeming with plant life likely dominate the landscape, with scorching deserts potentially existing in other regions, reflecting the Dendro element's connection to both growth and the harshness of nature. Sumeru's society revolves around scholarly pursuits, with its citizens likely deeply invested in research and the pursuit of wisdom. This focus on knowledge is mirrored in the playable characters like Alhaitham, and hints of artistic expression can be seen in characters like Nilou. The enemies encountered here might even be unique, with fungi-based creatures roaming the land. The architecture and themes draw inspiration from South Asian and Middle Eastern cultures, offering a glimpse into a world rich with history and cultural influence. Sumeru even boasts its own security force, the Eremites, who might play a role in the Traveler's adventures. With new mechanics focused on how Electro and Pyro elements interact with the abundant fungi, Sumeru promises to be a fascinating region unlike any other in Teyvat.
